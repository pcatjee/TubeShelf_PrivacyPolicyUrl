Privacy Policy

Last updated: [28th Jan 2026]

This Privacy Policy describes how Local YouTube Playlist Organizer ("the Extension", "we", "our", or "us") handles user data.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personally identifiable information.

Website Content Access

The Extension may access and process website content (such as page elements or text) only for the purpose of providing its core functionality.

All processing is performed locally within the user's browser. No website content is sent to external servers.

Personal Data

The Extension does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Authentication credentials or passwords
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • Personal communications

Local Storage

Any data saved by the Extension (such as user preferences or locally organized items) is stored only within the user's browser using local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves the user's device.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use third-party analytics tools, tracking services, advertising networks, or external APIs that collect user data.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of personal data exposure through this Extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
